Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 14, 2026

1. Overview

Sau Cua Kinh Reply Assistant is an AI-assisted productivity tool that helps TikTok creators manage comments, draft suggested replies, and review responses before manual posting.

2. Information We May Access

When a user grants permission through TikTok Login or related TikTok developer tools, the app may access basic TikTok account information and public video information permitted by the scopes approved by the user and TikTok.

The app may also process comment text or other content manually entered or imported by the user in order to generate suggested reply drafts.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information only to provide app functionality, including organizing creator content, preparing comment reply suggestions, and helping users review responses before posting.

The app does not automatically post comments, replies, or perform actions on TikTok without user review.

4. AI Processing

Comment text or other user-provided content may be processed by an AI service to generate suggested replies. AI-generated replies are suggestions only and should be reviewed, edited, and approved by the user before use.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. We do not share user data with third parties for advertising purposes. Data may be processed by service providers only when necessary to operate the app, such as AI processing or hosting services.

6. Data Storage

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Users may stop using the app at any time. If users want locally stored data removed, they may delete the app's local files or contact the app owner to request deletion of data associated with the service.

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for use by children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
